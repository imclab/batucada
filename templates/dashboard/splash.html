  • Knight-Mozilla  Open News

    shape the future of news

    Hack, learn and innovate with the world’s leading news organizations.

    • Drive open source innovation in news with the Knight Foundation and Mozilla
    • Learn and hack with news partners like the BBC, Guardian, Die Zeit, Al Jazeera and Boston Globe
    • Take part in learning labs and hack sprints around the globe
    • Apply to become a 2012 Knight-Mozilla News Fellow
    www.mozillaopennews.org

  • Mozilla Festival

    A yearly celebration that brings together hundreds of passionate people to explore the frontiers of the open web.

    • Developers, designers, and big thinkers together to make things that can change the world
    • Design challenges addressing key issues an ambitious chunk of prototyping and design
    • Replacing darkened lecture halls with maker labs full of people building things
    www.mozillafestival.org

  • Mozilla Hackasaurus

    webmaking made simple

    Hackasaurus makes it easy to create, tinker and share on the web.

    • Alter your online reality with the amazing X-Ray Goggles
    • Create your own web page in seconds
    • Host your own hack jam event for youth
    • Access teaching tools and kits for novice webmakers.
    www.hackasaurus.org

  • Mozilla Popcorn

    supercharge web video

    Popcorn adds interactivity and context to any online video, pulling the web right into the action in real time.

    • Explore what Mozilla Popcorn can do for film-makers, journalists and the classroom
    • Create your own interactive pop-up video
    • Get a taste of Popcorn through web documentaries, demos and more
    www.mozillapopcorn.org
